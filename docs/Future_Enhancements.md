# Future Enhancements

## Project
IT Service Request & Incident Management

---

## Planned Enhancements

### 1. SLA Management

Implement Service Level Agreements (SLAs) to monitor response and resolution times.

Benefits:
- Better service tracking
- Performance measurement
- Escalation management

---

### 2. CMDB Integration

Integrate Configuration Management Database (CMDB).

Benefits:
- Asset relationship tracking
- Impact analysis
- Improved incident management

---

### 3. Service Portal

Create a user-friendly self-service portal.

Benefits:
- Improved user experience
- Faster ticket creation
- Self-service capabilities

---

### 4. Knowledge Base Integration

Provide suggested knowledge articles during ticket creation.

Benefits:
- Reduced ticket volume
- Faster resolutions
- Improved support efficiency

---

### 5. Approval Workflow

Implement approval process for selected service requests.

Benefits:
- Governance
- Compliance
- Controlled request fulfillment

---

### 6. Reporting and Dashboards

Develop real-time dashboards and reports.

Benefits:
- Operational visibility
- KPI tracking
- Management reporting

---

### 7. Mobile Support

Enable ticket management through mobile devices.

Benefits:
- Increased accessibility
- Faster updates
- Better productivity

---

Transform the application into a complete IT Service Management (ITSM) solution supporting:
